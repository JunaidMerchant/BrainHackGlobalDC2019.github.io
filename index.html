Registration for the NIH Brainhack 2018 is now open!

The NIH Brainhack 2018 is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2018 that is organized by Adam Thomas . The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: May 2-3, 2018

Costs: FREE!

Location: Bethesda, MD, USA

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2018 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Registration for the San Francisco Brainhack 2017 is now open!

The San Francisco Brainhack 2017 is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2017 that is organized by Cameron Craddock . The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: March 03-05, 2017

Costs: $35 USD for students/postdocs/RAs, 65$ for faculty, includes onsite breakfast, lunch, and refreshments

Location: San Francisco, CA, USA

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2017 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.
